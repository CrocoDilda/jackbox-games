WHAT IS JACKBOX GAMES?

Jackbox Games is a Chicago-based studio renowned for its popular party games such as Quiplash, Fibbage, Drawful, Trivia Murder Party, and many more!

Imagine us as the offspring of traditional social games like 'charades'… the kind of offspring that left college early but eventually gained the family's respect by achieving global fame.

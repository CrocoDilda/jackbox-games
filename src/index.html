Frequently Asked Questions

Got More Questions?

We're here to help! If you can't find the answers you're seeking, take a look at our guide on How to Play.

  • Jackbox Games is a renowned studio that creates party games, famously known for titles such as YOU DON’T KNOW JACK, Quiplash, Fibbage, Drawful, and Trivia Murder Party. Imagine us as the quirky offspring of traditional party games like charades—only we dropped out of college and went on to revolutionize social gatherings with our game creations.

    You might have come across us through an article, a stream, an ad, or social media. Perhaps a friend introduced you to our games. Either way, welcome! Our goal is to bring a twist of fun and unpredictability to your parties and gatherings.

  • We offer a range of games designed to entertain everyone, whether they’re joining in person, on Zoom, or through a Twitch stream.

    From drawing and writing games to trivia and identity challenges, there’s something for everyone. Each year since 2014, we’ve rolled out a new set of games, compiled into a Jackbox Party Pack. Every pack contains 5 unique games that support 1-8 players. If you have a larger group, no worries—many of our games include audience participation, allowing additional players to join the fun.

    We also offer standalone titles beyond the Party Packs, such as Drawful 2.

  • Your friends and family just need a smartphone or any device with internet access. That’s right—no additional apps or purchases are necessary. They only need a mobile device, internet connection, and a way to view the game screen.

  • With your Party Pack purchased and your group assembled, starting the game is a breeze! Check out our video tutorial and detailed instructions on how to connect all players to the game. This guide also includes info on accessing extra features and customizing your game experience.

    Ensure everyone in your group, whether in-person or remote, can see and hear the game screen. This will allow them to enter the room code, participate in the game, and follow along with the action.

  • Visit our comprehensive support site for troubleshooting tips and solutions. You can also submit a support ticket, and our team aims to respond within 1-3 business days, though response times may vary during holidays.

    A quick tip: if a player’s device disconnects during the game, refreshing their web browser often resolves the issue and reconnects them instantly. If problems persist, let us know—our mission is to ensure no one misses out on the fun.
